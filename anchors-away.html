Anchors Away

Sail the Celestial Skies

Prepare to set sail on a high-seas adventure that will leave you completely breathless! Anchors Away is Enchanted Kingdom’s legendary giant pirate ship, built for daring buccaneers and travelers eager to experience the thrilling sensation of pure weightlessness.

Climb aboard our massive, beautifully crafted wooden galleon and secure your row. As the ship catches the mystical winds, it begins a massive pendulum swing, rocking back and forth higher and higher into the clouds. At its peak peak arc, you'll feel an unforgettable stomach-drop sensation as the vessel hangs in mid-air before plunging down to swing back up the other side!

Know Before You Go

  • Thrill Level High Magic, Massive Swinging, Gravity Defying Airtime
  • Height Requirement Minimum of 48 inches (122 cm)
  • Age Interest Teens, Adults, Brave Privateers
  • Accessibility Guests must transfer from wheelchair/ECV. Not recommended for those prone to motion sickness.
