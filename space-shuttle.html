Space Shuttle

Defy Gravity in the Realm

Prepare for a magical launch sequence that will send you hurtling through the celestial skies! The Space Shuttle is the Enchanted Kingdom's premier looping coaster, designed for the bravest of wizards and travelers.

You will be pulled backward to the top of a towering 11-story lift hill before being released. Plunge forward through a thrilling cobra roll and a massive vertical loop, only to repeat the entire heart-pounding journey in reverse! Experience the rush of wind and the thrill of weightlessness as you conquer the skies.

Know Before You Go

  • Thrill Level Extreme Magic, High Speed, Drops, Inversions
  • Height Requirement Minimum of 48 inches (122 cm)
  • Age Interest Teens, Adults, Brave Apprentices
  • Accessibility Guests must transfer from wheelchair/ECV.
