Jungle Log Jam

Brave the Rushing Waters

Prepare for a refreshing, wild expedition through the heart of the ancient jungle canopy! The Jungle Log Jam is one of the realm's most beloved aquatic adventures, offering an exhilarating escape for voyagers looking to cool down while keeping their adrenaline pumping.

Board your hollowed-out log vessel and navigate the winding, mystical waterways. You will ascend two separate, towering lift hills, each concluding with a heart-stopping, rapid plunge down a steep water flume into the splashing lagoons below. Brace yourself for a massive wave of excitement that is sure to leave you soaked and smiling!

Know Before You Go

  • Thrill Level Moderate Magic, Water Splashes, Steep Drops
  • Height Requirement Minimum of 42 inches (107 cm) with an adult / 48 inches (122 cm) alone
  • Age Interest Kids, Teens, Adventurous Families
  • Accessibility Guests must transfer from wheelchair/ECV. Expect to get wet.
