Flying Fiesta

Take Flight on the Magical Winds

Spread your wings and prepare for a joyful flight that will send you soaring above the bustling pathways of the realm! The Flying Fiesta is Enchanted Kingdom's premier wave swinger, combining the pure bliss of a playground swing with the majestic scale of a high-flying aerial adventure.

Take your seat in one of the beautifully suspended chairs and hold on tight as the grand canopy begins to spin. As you gain speed, the entire ride lifts up and gently tilts, letting the mystical centripetal forces take over. Feel the crisp wind rush past your face as you glide through the air, enjoying a breathtaking, panoramic bird's-eye view of the kingdom below!

Know Before You Go

  • Thrill Level Moderate Magic, High-Flying, Gentle Tilting
  • Height Requirement Minimum of 42 inches (107 cm) with an adult / 48 inches (122 cm) alone
  • Age Interest Kids, Teens, Open-Air Explorers
  • Accessibility Guests must transfer from wheelchair/ECV. Secure all loose items and footwear before taking flight.
